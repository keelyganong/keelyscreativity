The Craft

I love creating a product. Whether it's in a physical sense with my crochet hook and ball of yarn or a digital sense with a mind full of ideas, I'm excited at the opportunity to impact others with something that I can produce with my unique skills.

In an Entrepreneurship course last January, I was tasked with making an operating E-Commerce experience. The established site I made alongside my classmate was an idea we brought to reality with personalized marketing, branding, and e-commerce capabilities We went a step further than the class requirements and decided to actually create and sell the physical products. Not only successful, this project drove me to pursue my passions in a purposeful way and connect with others through a product experience.

Clothing Creation

My latest endeavor and the culmination of my passions for creative design, innovation, nature, and experience: Shop Indigo Designs. This is a brand I established to inspire sustainable clothing consumption and connect a community through an inspirational good.

The product I create is one-of-a-kind wearable art. Dyed in natural indigo, printed with hand-cut stamps, stitched with vintage fabric patches, each piece truly tells a story - something I love to do. View my creations above and on my online storefront.
